The criminals that got away!

Advent is a group of criminals that escaped from The Cell. It consists of Shiori Novella, Nerissa Ravencroft, Koseki Bijou, Fuwawa Abyssgard, and Mococo Abyssgard, who were all imprisoned for various reasons. However, they escaped from The Cell to achieve one shared goal: to rebel against the world, their destinies, and their monotonous lives.
